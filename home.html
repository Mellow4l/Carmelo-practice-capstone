Air Pollution In Oakland

How does it affect you and your health?

Types of air pollution

Black Carbon

Black carbon particles come from burning fuel, especially diesel, wood and coal. High exposure is associated with heart attacks, stroke and some forms of cancer.

Nitric oxide (NO)

Nitric oxide is strongly associated with heavy traffic, and is often concentrated near busy roadways. It forms smog and acid rain, and can cause respiratory problems.

Nitrogen dioxide (NO2)

Nitrogen dioxide is formed when NO(Nitric oxide) mixes with oxygen in the air. It's associated with respiratory problems,and is regulated by the EPA( Environmental Protection Agency).
